## 🧮 Formula ##

$$P(x) = _nC_xp²q^{x-n}$$

$$P(x) = \frac{n!}{(n-x)!x!}*p^xq^{n-x}$$

$$n = \mbox{Number of trials}$$

$$x = \mbox{Number of success desired}$$

$$p = \mbox{Probability of getting sucess in one trial}$$

$$q = 1-p = \mbox{Probability of getting a failure in on trial}$$
